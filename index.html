Who Am I?

My name is Spencer Hayes and I'm a Desktop Support Specialist at Boston University working out of the Graduate Center for Integrated Life Sciences and Engineering complex. My day-to-day consists of providing overarching support for various research labs and research compute configurations, the majority of which are Linux-based. This work includes managing custom Linux servers, computing clusters, job queues, as well as asset management software such as KACE and ePO. I hold a B.A. in Computer Science, and am looking to pursue an M.S. soon.
