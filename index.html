Who Am I?

My name is Spencer Hayes and I'm a Technical Operations Engineer at Instaclustr working out of Cambridge, Massachusetts. I graduated from Boston University in 2018 with a B.A. in Computer Science and a minor in Japanese Language & Literature. My day-to-day consists of monitoring, administrating, and triaging clusters on our managed platform. This primarily includes administrating various Apache Cassandra, Kafka, and Spark clusters in a variety of configurations and states.

Strengths and Interests

Systems Administration has always been a strong interest of mine, particularly in the context of distributed systems and cloud computing. I greatly enjoy working with new technology and pride myself on being able to quickly adapt to changing environments. Java, Python, and Bash are my most prolific languages, under which I have years of experience working with various libraries/frameworks, database systems, stacks, and tooling. Outside of the world of tech, some things I enjoy are running, aviation, playing saxophone, and chess.
